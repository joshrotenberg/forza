## Approach

Both `claude-wrapper` and `codex-wrapper` expose a `RetryPolicy` type (re-exported at the crate root) and a `.retry(RetryPolicy)` method on their respective client builders (`Claude::builder()` and `Codex::builder()`). The fix is to call `.retry(RetryPolicy::new().exponential().retry_on_timeout(true))` on each builder before `.build()`, enabling up to 3 attempts with exponential backoff (1s→2s→4s, capped at 30s) on timeout errors. This catches transient CLI failures—timeouts, process crashes, overload—inside the wrapper layer without re-running the full forza stage. No new fields, traits, or config surface are needed.

In `executor.rs`, the change is in `ClaudeAdapter::execute_stage` where `Claude::builder()` is constructed. In `adapters.rs`, the change is in `CodexAgentAdapter::execute` where `Codex::builder()` is constructed.
